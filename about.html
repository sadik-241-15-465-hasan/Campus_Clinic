Mission & Vision

Mission: Our mission is to simplify medical access for university students and staff with an intuitive platform backed by real-time support and secure medical data management.

Vision: To become the benchmark for digital campus healthcare systems globally, starting with our flagship project in collaboration with Dubai partners.

Welfare Activities

We organize health camps, awareness programs, and mental health sessions across the campus. These include free check-ups, blood donation drives, and COVID-19 precautions in collaboration with local health experts.

Quality Policy

DIU Medicare follows a strict quality assurance policy ensuring each medical case is handled securely and swiftly. We implement encryption protocols and regular audits to maintain platform reliability and data integrity.

Present Unit

We are currently operating inside the DIU Smart Campus with dedicated rooms for consultation, patient waiting, and emergency response. The app complements offline services with appointment management and instant chat support.

Ongoing Project (Dubai)

We are actively partnering with healthcare stakeholders in Dubai to adapt DIU Medicare's system for multi-language support and international integration. The pilot launch is expected in mid-2025.
